💎 The Three Pillars

Every pitch maps to at least one of these. Hit the pillar(s) that match their signal.

🔗 Reliability

Model fallback chains: no outage when a provider goes dark. Traffic auto-routes before users notice latency.

Who cares: Anyone with customer-facing AI

🛡️ Security

PII Shield + Tool-Call Firewall + compliance recipes (SOC2, HIPAA). Blocks secrets before they hit the LLM.

Who cares: Enterprise, Fintech, Healthcare

💰 Cost

Auto-route simple tasks to cheaper models. Zero-markup passthrough. Save 30-50% on inference.

Who cares: High-volume API consumers

🔍 Discovery & Objections

Discovery Call Structure (15 min)

0-2m: Open

"I reached out because [specific signal]. We've been helping companies in your exact position ship multi-model AI safely."

2-5m: Diagnostic

1. When [primary model] degrades, what's your failover?
2. Any PII/PHI/secrets concerns?
3. What are you spending on inference?

10-13m: The Close

"We typically do a 90-day POC, monthly payments. If it doesn't deliver by day 90, you walk. Sound worth exploring?"

Objection Handling

"We built failover internally"

→ "How are you handling degraded performance vs. full outage? What about partial failures where the model returns garbage?"

"Security team won't approve a proxy"

→ "We deploy in your VPC. Data never leaves your perimeter. We're the firewall, not a man-in-the-middle."

"Too early — still building features"

→ "This is the right time. It's harder to bolt on compliance and fallback after you've shipped."
