Playbook Overview

Three Core Pillars

Every pitch maps to at least one of these.

Reliability

Model fallback chains: no outage when a provider goes dark. Traffic auto-routes before users notice latency.

Who cares: Anyone with customer-facing AI

Security

PII Shield + Tool-Call Firewall + compliance recipes (SOC2, HIPAA). Blocks secrets before they hit the LLM.

Who cares: Enterprise, Fintech, Healthcare

Cost

Auto-route simple tasks to cheaper models. Zero-markup passthrough. Save 30-50% on inference.

Who cares: High-volume API consumers

Outreach Sequencing

Week 1: Hot + High Urgency

Replit - Call then email same day. Reference Claude Code integration.

Sourcegraph - Lead with tool-call firewall.

Intercom - Lead with PII in support tickets.

Box - Lead with HIPAA + PII Shield.

Week 2: Hot + Medium Urgency

Cursor - Same play as Replit. Notion - Claude outage angle. GitLab - Secrets in code prompts + SOC2. Databricks - Platform-scale cost optimization.

Week 3: Warm

Benchling, Plaid, Temporal, Grafana, Confluent, LaunchDarkly, Sentry, Figma.

Messaging Frameworks

Framework A: The Trigger (Call Version)

"Hey [Name] - I saw you are hiring [AI platform / agent infra] engineers and shipping multi-model AI features. My name is [Rep], I am with FlashLabs. We built OrcaRouter to solve the exact problem your job posts describe: model availability, tool-call security, and cost at scale. Could I get 15 minutes to show you how it works?"

Framework B: The Pattern-Break (Email)

Subject: Your [Claude/GPT-4o] users do not care whose model answers. They care that it works. [Name] - When [Provider X] has degraded performance, your AI features break. Your users do not blame Anthropic. They blame [Company]. OrcaRouter sits between your app and every LLM provider with intelligent fallback chains - traffic auto-routes to the next-best model before your users notice latency. 15 minutes to show you the stack? I can also show you the PII Shield for [specific compliance need]. [Rep]

Framework C: The Cost Conversation (LinkedIn/Email)

Subject: 30-50% on inference without touching quality [Name] - Every AI code completion / document analysis / support response is hitting an expensive model by default. The simple ones (syntax, Q and A) do not need Claude 4. OrcaRouter auto-classifies requests and routes simple ones to cheaper models. Zero quality loss on the complex stuff. No markup on API calls - we are a passthrough, not a proxy adding margin. Worth 15 minutes?

Discovery Call Structure (15 min)

0-2m: Open

"I reached out because [specific signal]. We have been helping companies in your exact position ship multi-model AI safely."

2-5m: Diagnostic

1. When [primary model] degrades, what is your failover?
2. Any PII/PHI/secrets concerns?
3. What are you spending on inference?

10-13m: The Close

"We typically do a 90-day POC, monthly payments. If it does not deliver by day 90, you walk. Sound worth exploring?"

Objection Handling

"We built failover internally"
-> "How are you handling degraded performance vs. full outage? What about partial failures where the model returns garbage?"

"Security team wont approve a proxy"
-> "We deploy in your VPC. Data never leaves your perimeter. We are the firewall, not a man-in-the-middle."

"Too early - still building features"
-> "This is the right time. It is harder to bolt on compliance and fallback after you have shipped."
